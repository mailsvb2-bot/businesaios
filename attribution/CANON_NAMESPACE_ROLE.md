# attribution namespace role

`attribution/` is the canonical attribution and provenance surface.

It owns:
- touchpoint interpretation
- campaign-to-revenue linking explanations
- first-touch / last-touch / multi-touch models
- lead-to-revenue resolution views
- attribution audit outputs

Must NOT contain:
- second mutable lead outcome registry
- writable business-outcome state truth
- canonical outcome timeline mutation
- second lead status / contacted / converted storage layer
- direct registry-backed outcome persistence ownership
